# Cowboy Cookies

Vermeer family stand by cookie.  We had these in our school lunches almost every day!

```
Oven temperature: 350F
Baking time: 12 minutes
```

## Ingredients

- 1 c soft butter or other shortening
- 1 c brown sugar
- 1 c sugar
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 c sifted flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 12 oz package chocolate chips
- 2 c oats
- 1/2 c nuts (optional)


## Instructions

- Cream the shortening
- Add the sugar and cream more
- Add eggs and vanilla and mix thoroughly
- Add flour soda, baking powder and salt and mix until incorporated
- Add oats, chocolate chips, and nuts
- Drop 2" apart onto ungreased cookie sheet
- Bake about 12 minutes at 350F
- Remove from baking sheet immediately onto a rack

## Notes

- The traditional chocolate chip is semi-sweet, but these work great with milk chocolate chips as well
